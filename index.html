It is a privilege to welcome you to the Balkan Osstem osseointegration meeting 2026 (BOOM 26), to be held in the beautiful multicultural city of Sarajevo from 00-00 Month 2026.

In Sarajevo, we will gather at a time of rapid change in science, technology, and society. Dentistry must not only keep pace with these changes but help shape them. This Meeting will provide an exceptional platform to explore the latest research, innovations, and clinical practices, while also engaging in meaningful dialogue about the future of our profession.
